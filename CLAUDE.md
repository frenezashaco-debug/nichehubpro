## ARTICLE GENERATION RULES
Every article generated by publisher_v2.py must follow these 12 rules exactly:

### 1. TITLE
- Include a number
- Include the primary keyword in the first words
- Make it catchy and emotional

### 2. INTRO
- Hook the reader emotionally
- Include the primary keyword in the first 10 words
- Short paragraphs (max 2-3 lines each)
- 3 paragraphs total

### 3. TL;DR SECTION
- Add immediately after intro
- 2-3 sentences only
- Must include the primary keyword
- Must directly answer the article topic

### 4. STRUCTURE — Question-Based H2 Headings
Use exactly these heading types:
- What is [topic]?
- What are the signs?
- Why does it happen?
- How to fix it?
- How to manage [topic] daily? (or equivalent 5th section)

### 5. CONTENT STYLE
- Short paragraphs (max 3 lines)
- Use bullet points in every section
- Bold key advice
- At least one real-life example (2 separate paragraphs, NOT merged into one)
- Must sound like a real human wrote it

### 6. GEO OPTIMIZATION (apply to every paragraph)
Each paragraph must follow this exact structure:
1. Start with a clear, direct statement
2. Include a fact, statistic, or explanation
3. End with practical, actionable advice

### 7. INTERNAL LINKING
- 3-5 internal links naturally placed inside the article
- Descriptive keyword anchor text only (NEVER "click here")
- Each anchor text must be unique (never repeat the same anchor)
- Suggest relevant related topics such as:
  - how to stop overthinking
  - how to calm anxiety quickly
  - daily habits to reduce stress
  - morning routine for mental health
  - how to build discipline

### 8. FAQ SECTION
- 5 real questions people search on Google about this topic
- Answers: clear, short, useful
- Must be formatted as accordion items

### 9. CONCLUSION
- Motivational tone
- Encourage one small action today
- 3 short paragraphs
- Human and warm

### 10. DISCLAIMER
Always add at the end:
"This article is for informational purposes only and does not replace professional medical advice."

### 11. COVER IMAGE RULES (VERY IMPORTANT)

**Generation:**
- Generate a UNIQUE concept for every single article — no two covers can look alike
- The image must visually represent the specific article topic, not the category in general
- Describe in full detail: subject, environment, action, lighting, mood, style

**Subject:**
- Always a real person (relatable, not a model — real human energy)
- Show an emotion tied to the article topic (stress, calm, focus, fatigue, hope)
- Natural, unposed body language

**Environment:**
- Wellness settings: bedroom, kitchen, nature path, home office, yoga space
- Clean, minimal, no clutter
- Matches the mood of the article

**Lighting:**
- Soft natural window light preferred
- Warm and calming tones
- Slightly blurred background (shallow depth of field)

**Style:**
- Photorealistic wellness lifestyle photography
- Could be mistaken for a real professional photo
- High resolution, sharp focus, 4K quality
- Warm color palette

**STRICT RULES:**
- NEVER include text, words, watermarks, logos, or graphic overlays
- NEVER use stock-photo poses (arms crossed, fake smile)
- NEVER duplicate a concept used in a previous article
- NEVER use dark, cold, or clinical tones
- Each cover must feel like a different scene, different person, different moment

### 12. WRITING RULES (NEVER BREAK)
- NEVER use em dashes (—) anywhere
- NEVER use the word "delve"
- NEVER write "In today's fast-paced world", "It is important to note", "In conclusion"
- NEVER repeat the same anchor text for internal links
- Keep paragraphs SHORT (2-3 lines max)
- Simple, clear English — write like a knowledgeable friend
- Meta description: exactly 155-160 characters, includes primary keyword
