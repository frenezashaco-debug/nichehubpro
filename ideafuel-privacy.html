Privacy Policy – IdeaFuel: Habit & Focus Timer

Effective date: April 21, 2026

IdeaFuel ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the IdeaFuel mobile application ("App") handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

IdeaFuel does not require you to create an account. Most data (habits, focus sessions, XP, badges, streaks) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

The following third-party services are used and may collect data:

  • Google AdMob – displays banner advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics – collects anonymous usage events (e.g. app open, habit completed, focus session finished) to help us improve the App. No personally identifiable information is collected. See Firebase Privacy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To understand how users interact with the App via Firebase Analytics
  • To improve App features and user experience

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party.

3. Data Storage

All habit data, focus sessions, XP points, streaks, and badges are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete this data.

4. Children's Privacy

IdeaFuel is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

5. Push Notifications

The App may send local push notifications to remind you of your daily habits and focus sessions. These notifications are generated on-device and do not transmit any data externally. You can disable them at any time in your device settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
