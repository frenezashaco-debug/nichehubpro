About Sarah

Writing that actually helps

Sarah Mitchell has spent 7 years writing about mental health, anxiety, productivity, and healthy living. With a background in psychology and a personal history of managing chronic overthinking, she brings both professional knowledge and lived experience to every article.

Her approach is simple: take complex research from institutions like the APA, NIMH, and WHO, and translate it into clear, honest advice that people can actually use. No filler, no scare tactics, no vague encouragement.

Every article she writes goes through the same process: verify the research, get the structure right, make sure the conclusion gives you something real to do. Because the goal isn't just to inform — it's to help.
